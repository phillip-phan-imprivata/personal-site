SketchBook

Single-page React Application

SketchBook is an app that gives users a creative outlet by allowing users to design and edit drawings made on the app. The "SketchPad" page presents users with an input to choose the size of the sketch. Once the SketchPad is generated, users are able to design their sketch by drawing, erasing, and choosing various colors. Users are also able to save the sketch to their "SketchBook". On the "SketchBook" page, users are able to view all of their drawings as well as edit them individually. SketchBook is a fun alternative to pen and paper for the average doodler.
